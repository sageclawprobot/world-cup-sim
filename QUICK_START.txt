🚀 Quick Start (3 Commands)
============================

1. Fetch live data & predict:
   cd ~/world-cup-sim/src
   python3 main.py --live

2. Use cached data (faster):
   python3 main.py

3. Run tests:
   python3 test_models.py
   python3 test_predictor.py

📈 Prediction Formula
======================

Composite Score = (Squad Score × 0.6) + (League Strength × 0.4)

Squad Score      = Avg(top 7 starters) with weighted metrics
League Strength  = W/D/L record + goal difference

Win Probability  = Team A Score / Total Score × 100
